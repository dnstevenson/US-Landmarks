This app contains the current National Historic Landmarks and National Memorials of the United States. The information is from Wikipedia.org and may not be 100% accurate. Information and images are courtesy of Wikipedia and their respective authors. Please visit Wikipedia and our website for links to images used in the application.

  • The "States" list is to browse the National Landmarks and National Memorials by state.
  • While browsing the list by State, you can click the upper-right icon to map all the landmarks in that state.
  • The "Map" icon shows you Landmarks and Memorials near your current Location. You can tap the red pins to display the landmark's information. You can also slide the map around to show landmarks all across the country.
  • The "Star" icon in the upper right corner of a Landmark lets you mark that landmark as a Favorite. This is a quick and easy way to remember landmarks you've visited or would like to visit
